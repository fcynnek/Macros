Scenario

Susan serves as a field security coordination officer in a United Nations field mission. She is responsible for managing the physical security of United Nations premises called “Camp Zulu”.

Camp Zulu has a fully-fledged CCTV system installed, which are monitored from a CCTV control panel located at the Security Control Centre.

One Saturday night, Susan visited the Security Control Centre where the designated surveillance officer was monitoring the CCTV panels. The surveillance officer asked if Susan would monitor the CCTV while she went to the bathroom.

While sitting in front of the panels, Susan notices Jo and Mary, two of her colleagues kissing and becoming increasingly intimate on one of the cameras monitoring a secluded area. Susan knows that both Jo and Mary have spouses back home and have met both at work functions.

By the time the surveillance officer returns, Jo and Mary had moved on.

What should Susan do with this information?{mlang}

Le temps que l'agente de surveillance revienne, Jo et Mary sont parties.

Que devrait faire Susan ?{mlang}
